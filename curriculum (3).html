Full curriculum

A structured progression from letters to full sentences — taught through games, Kannada explanations, and examples from Karnataka life. Every lesson is free.

Part 1 — Beginner
Short vowel word families, sight words, and simple sentences. Designed for Grade 1–3 students with little or no prior English.
